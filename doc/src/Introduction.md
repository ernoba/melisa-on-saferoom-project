# Introduction

**MELISA PROJECT**, also known as *Management Environment Linux Sandbox*, is a development environment management project written in **Rust**. It is designed to create isolated, lightweight, and high-performance workspaces.

The project focuses on:
- **Ease of collaboration** and project management.
- **Solving age-old developer issues** such as "Host Pollution" and the infamous "It Works on My Machine" syndrome.

*Simply put*, **MELISA** acts as a "manager" that automatically prepares, configures, and secures a sandbox (isolated container) within a Linux system. This allows you to perform coding experiments freely without the risk of damaging your main (host) system.
